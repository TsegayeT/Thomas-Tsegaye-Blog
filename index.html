Tatum Stats Distribution

Tatum stats having been going in the right direction over his career. His Points, TS, and eFG have a wider distribution in 2022 season because
he started off shooting bad in the first few months of the season. Tatum picked it up in Jan 2022 and has been on fire since. He is now a top 5 MVP
candidate. The encouraging sign is that his USG has increased a lot but his eFG and TS hasnt dropped. This shows he can be a heliocentric player,
having the offense run through him. On top of that his Assists and Total Rebounding are increasing, making him a complete player. He is also a great
defender, on and off ball. Click on the header for the code in my Github.
